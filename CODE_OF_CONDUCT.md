# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a welcoming and constructive environment.

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Focus on technical merit when reviewing contributions
- Accept and give constructive feedback gracefully

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
