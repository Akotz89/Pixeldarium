# Pixel Sim Engine - Slice 1

## How to run

1. Put `index.html`, `style.css`, and `game.js` in the same folder.
2. Double-click `index.html`.
3. It should open in your browser.

## What exists now

* A canvas-based pixel world
* Organisms that wander
* Food that appears
* Organisms eat food
* Organisms reproduce when they have enough energy
* Organisms die when they run out of energy or get too old

## Requirements

No Node.js required.
No npm.
No libraries.
